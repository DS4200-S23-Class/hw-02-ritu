Task Abstraction

High-level: Present; the graph is showing the viewer something specific.

Medium-level: Lookup; the target (maximum temperature in the last 3 days) and the target's location is known.

Low-level: Compare; the graph shows how the maximum temperature in the last 3 days is higher than the maximum temperature on the same days from 1979-2021.

Target(s): All data --> Outliers; the graph bolds the maximum temperatures for the last 3 days since they deviate from the trend of daily maximum temperatures.

High-level: Discover; the viewer must explore the visualization in order to find something meaningful.

Medium-level: Lookup; the targets (Seattle and Portland) and the targets' location is known.

Low-level: Compare; displays two timelines with the same information for Seattle and for Portland.

Target(s): Attributes --> Extremes; only counts one attribute that lies outside of normality (number of times daily mean temperatures were 20 degrees F above normal).
